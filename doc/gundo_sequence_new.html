Creates a new, empty GundoSequence.

The GundoSequence is destroyed by a call to gtk_object_destroy. All of the actions in a sequence are destroyed (by calling the free callback of their GundoActionType) when the sequence is destroyed.

Returns:
A new GundoSequence object.
